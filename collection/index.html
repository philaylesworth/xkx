My Virtual Motorcycle Collection

I would love to have a garage full of motorcycles. The only problem is that I only ride about 10,000km a year. Oh yeah, I really don't enjoy tinkering with old bikes, either. And, I really like how newer, fuel injected bikes just start, no matter how cold it is. So, I am not a collector but that doesn't mean I don't lust for every cool bike I see.

This virtual collection is my answer to that dilemma. I can have all the bikes I desire, stored in one safe place, and I never have to service or wash them!

This site is an experiment in using Google Sheets for the backend data.
